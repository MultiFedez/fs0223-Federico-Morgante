Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow you are taste buds this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries,and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus morre for the pan
    For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onion,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
    In a mediu size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
